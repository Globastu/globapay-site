Building the Plumbing for Global Value Transfer

We're creating the infrastructure that enables seamless, compliant, and efficient payments across every corner of the global economy

Our Mission

To democratize access to global payment infrastructure, enabling platforms worldwide to accept, process, and settle payments with the same ease as the world's largest companies.

Our Vision

A world where geographical boundaries don't limit business opportunities, where any platform can serve customers globally with seamless, instant, and compliant payment experiences.

Our Approach

We combine traditional financial infrastructure with cutting-edge Web3 technology to create payment rails that are both reliable and innovative, serving markets others can't reach.

The Story Behind Globapay

2020

The Problem Identified

Our founders experienced firsthand the complexity and limitations of existing payment infrastructure while building platforms in frontier markets. The gap between what global businesses needed and what was available was stark.

2021

Foundation Laid

Started building the core infrastructure with a focus on compliance-first architecture and Web3 integration. Early partnerships with payment processors and regulatory bodies in key markets.

2022

First Platforms Onboarded

Launched with select partners in insurance, fintech, and e-commerce sectors. Proved the concept with successful cross-border settlements in Africa and Latin America.

2023

Product Suite Expansion

Launched Globagift and Globainvoice, demonstrating the power of our unified infrastructure approach. Expanded to cover 50+ local markets with specialized payment methods.

2024

Global Scale Achieved

Supporting 700+ payment methods across 200+ markets. Enabling billions in payment volume with industry-leading success rates and settlement speeds.
