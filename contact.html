Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we integrate Globapay?

Most integrations are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. We provide dedicated technical support and comprehensive documentation to streamline the process.

What's your pricing model?

We offer transparent, volume-based pricing with no setup fees. Pricing varies by region and payment methods. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your needs.

Do you support sandbox testing?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive sandbox environment that mirrors production capabilities, allowing you to test all features before going live.

What compliance certifications do you have?

We're PCI DSS Level 1 certified, SOC 2 Type II compliant, and maintain regulatory compliance in all markets we operate in, including PSD2, GDPR, and local regulations.

Can you handle high transaction volumes?

Yes, our infrastructure is built to scale. We currently process billions in payment volume and can auto-scale to handle traffic spikes and growth.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 technical support, dedicated account management for enterprise clients, comprehensive documentation, and implementation assistance.
